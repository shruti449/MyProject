Information about the internship position

I joined Endurance company as an intern to learn about PLC working in maintainance section under the department of Electronics and telecommunication.

Description of the internship experience

I completed my internship of 20 days at Endurance company under PLC working unit. I introduced to SCADA and HMI along with the working of PLC using software as well as hardware. I learnt about ‘PLC Ladder logic’ i.e. PLC programming using simulator named ‘GX Works2’. The GX Works2 package supports entire engineering such as system design, programming, debug and maintenance. I got some tasks to complete everyday using PLC ladder logic and I really enjoyed learning and performing it.

Conclusion        

From my internship at Endurance, I was able to get a better understanding of how the manufacturing industry works and how effective it is.Overall, I found the internship experience to be positive, and I am sure I would be able to use the skills I learned in my career later.
